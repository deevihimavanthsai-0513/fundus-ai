Understanding Diabetic Retinopathy

Diabetic Retinopathy (DR) is a diabetes complication that affects the eyes. It’s caused by damage to the blood vessels of the light-sensitive tissue at the back of the eye (retina). Early detection is essential to prevent vision loss. Our system uses a trained Deep Learning model to classify DR into stages such as Mild, Moderate, Severe, and Proliferative.

Explainable AI with Grad-CAM

To ensure clinical transparency, our model integrates Grad-CAM (Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping) — a visualization technique that highlights the specific regions in the retina that influenced the AI’s decision. This helps clinicians trust the AI results and understand the reasoning behind every classification.
